Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about partnering with Xenotrix Technologies for your digital transformation journey.

What makes Xenotrix Technologies different from other IT companies?

Xenotrix operates with an AI-first development philosophy — every solution we build incorporates intelligent automation, predictive capabilities, or data intelligence as a core feature, not an afterthought. Combined with our structured enterprise delivery process, dedicated account management, and commitment to measurable ROI, we deliver outcomes that generic agencies and freelancers simply cannot match.

Do you work with startups or only large enterprises?

We are designed for both. Our solutions are architected to scale — meaning a startup can launch a lean MVP today and expand to enterprise-grade infrastructure tomorrow without rebuilding from scratch. We serve funded startups, SMEs, and large enterprises with equal dedication, and we price our engagements accordingly.

What technologies does Xenotrix specialize in?

Our core stack includes React, Next.js, Flutter, FastAPI, Django, Python, PostgreSQL, Redis, and cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, Azure). On the AI side, we work with OpenAI APIs, Hugging Face models, TensorFlow, PyTorch, and LangChain for LLM application development. We also have deep expertise in data engineering with tools like Apache Spark, dbt, and Tableau/Power BI.

How do you ensure project quality and on-time delivery?

We follow a structured 4-phase delivery process: Discovery & Strategy, Architecture & Design, Agile Development, and Launch & Scale. Every project includes automated testing, code review, CI/CD pipelines, and bi-weekly sprint reviews with full client transparency. Our dedicated project managers ensure scope, timeline, and budget stay aligned throughout the engagement.
