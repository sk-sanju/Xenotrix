Customer Relationship Management Platform

XenoCRM

XenoCRM is a powerful, conversion-focused CRM platform built to help businesses capture more leads, close deals faster, and manage customer relationships with precision. Developed using Django and MySQL, it gives sales teams a centralized workspace to track prospects, automate follow-ups, monitor pipeline movement, and turn everyday interactions into measurable revenue growth.

  • Category: CRM Platform
  • Tech Stack: Django, MySQL, and Frontend Technologies
  • Business Impact: Lead Conversion, Pipeline Visibility, Follow-up Automation
  • Ideal For: Sales teams, business owners, client relationship managers
  • Deployment: Private CRM Solution for Growth-Focused Businesses

From lead acquisition to deal closure, XenoCRM is designed to accelerate sales operations, improve follow-up consistency, and give businesses full control over their customer pipeline through a fast, responsive, and analytics-driven CRM environment.

Financial Operations System

XenoFin

A comprehensive finance management and cash flow ledger. XenoFin streamlines invoice tracking, revenue reconciliation, business expenses, automated partner payout calculations, and tax report compliance in a secure environment.

  • Category: Financial Management Software
  • Tech Stack: Django, Python, PostgreSQL, ChartJS
  • Key Features: Partner payouts, automated invoicing, cash flow forecasting
  • Project URL: Visit XenoFin Website

Built on a secure Django framework with a highly optimized PostgreSQL transaction log, XenoFin helps businesses mitigate errors and track margins in real time.
