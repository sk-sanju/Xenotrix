Your Website Is Your Most Powerful Business Asset

A Xenotrix-built web platform is not a digital brochure — it's a high-performance revenue engine. We combine cutting-edge frontend technology, intelligent backend architecture, and data-driven UX to build web systems that convert visitors into customers and customers into advocates.

What We Build

Corporate & Enterprise Portals

Professional corporate websites and enterprise portals with CMS integration, multi-language support, accessibility compliance, and SEO-optimized architecture that positions your brand as an industry leader.

E-Commerce Platforms

High-conversion online stores with smart product recommendations, seamless payment gateways (Razorpay, Stripe), inventory management, and analytics dashboards designed to maximize revenue per visitor.

SaaS Web Applications

Full-featured SaaS dashboards with multi-tenant user management, subscription billing, real-time data visualization, role-based access control, and API integrations that form the beating heart of your product.

Progressive Web Apps (PWA)

Web applications with native-like capabilities — offline mode, push notifications, app-like installation — that deliver mobile-grade experiences without the app store friction, driving higher engagement rates.

Our Web Development Process

01
Technical Discovery & Architecture Planning

We analyze your business requirements, user personas, traffic projections, and integration needs to define the optimal technical stack and system architecture before any development begins.

02
UI/UX Design & Prototype Validation

High-fidelity Figma prototypes of all key pages and user flows are built and validated with stakeholders before development. Zero rework surprises.

03
Agile Development in 2-Week Sprints

Sprint-based delivery with client review at every milestone. Automated tests, code review pipelines, and staging environments ensure quality throughout.

04
Performance Optimization & SEO Integration

Every Xenotrix web platform is optimized for Core Web Vitals (LCP, CLS, FID), semantic SEO structure, and schema markup before launch — ranking and converting from day one.

05
Deployment, Monitoring & Ongoing Support

Cloud deployment with uptime monitoring, automated backups, security patching, and ongoing performance reporting. We remain your technology partner post-launch.
