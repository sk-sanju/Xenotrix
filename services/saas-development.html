Architecting the Future of Software

Building a successful SaaS requires more than a standard web app. It demands robust tenant isolation, scalable recurring billing, real-time analytics, and high-performance server clusters. We combine cloud-native engineering with agile product design to deliver SaaS platforms that convert and retain users.

Our SaaS Development Services

Multi-Tenant Database Architectures

Secure and logical partitioning of tenant data (shared DB with schema isolation or separate databases) ensuring bulletproof security, high performance, and simple migration policies as your user base scales.

Subscription & Metred Billing

Integrations with major global payment networks (Stripe, Razorpay, PayPal) supporting complex tier structures, metred billing based on usage, automatic webhooks for failed cards, and seamless upgrades/downgrades.

Centralized Admin Dashboards

Custom control centers for platform administrators to monitor registrations, audit system logs, manage user accounts, resolve billing issues, and trace server performance in real time.

Metrics & Funnel Analytics

Built-in tracking for SaaS health metrics: MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue), LTV (Lifetime Value), Churn Rate, Active Users (DAU/MAU), and marketing conversion funnels to drive smart product optimization.

Our Core Products

Enterprise SaaS Solutions: XenoCRM & XenoFin

Xenotrix is the engineering force behind successful B2B SaaS platforms. Our flagship products include **XenoCRM** (a high-performance sales pipeline and client registry system built with React and FastAPI) and **XenoFin** (a secure Django-based financial operations ledger and payout system). Our next product, **XenoHR** (smart attendance, leaves, and payroll manager), is launching soon.
